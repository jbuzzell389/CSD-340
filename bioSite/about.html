Full Bio

Henry Peprah was born as a fraternal twin a few decades ago in Ghana, West Africa. Their mother, the now late Elizabeth Peprah, delivered the twins by herself. The other twin goes by the name of Jerald Peprah. They are currently roommates in Omaha, NE. Henry is one of nine, and there are several step siblings within the family as well. Paul Peprah, the father, had five wives at the time, hence why such a massive family. Having multiple partners is more socially acceptable in Africa.

Henry’s eldest sister, Emelia, met a gentleman by the name of Jim Buzzell. He was a member of the Peace Corps was sent to Ghana to help with the family’s village. He taught mathematics there and eventually married Emelia, and he brought her to the US. This prompted Henry, along with many other siblings, to move to the US. Henry ended up in North Carolina, and he met a woman name Vivian. They had two kids together, named Zion and Rubianna. Zion and Rubianna are now two smart young adults attending college.

Henry and Vivian decided to go their separate ways, and Henry moved to Omaha for a fresh start. Not too long after moving, he graduated from Strayer University with a bachelor’s degree in Database Management. He worked at a company called Alorica for 10+ years, and is now attending school full-time, studying Cybersecurity. Henry’s hobbies include music, reading, and spending time with family.

Henry hopes to become a billionaire someday working in the tech industry. He is in the process of earning his master’s degree and hopes to land a nice job after he graduates. He also wants to eventually travel more. We will see if he can get a chance to see what the rest of the world has to offer.
